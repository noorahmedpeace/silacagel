## SECTION 6 — Jaise-jaise profiles bante jayen, mujhe link do

Jab aap Google Business, LinkedIn, Facebook, Alibaba profiles bana lo, unke **URLs mujhe bhej dena**. Main website ki Organization structured-data mein `sameAs` links add kar dunga — isse Google in profiles ko aap ke brand se officially jodta hai (extra trust + "knowledge panel" ka chance).

Chahiye ye format mein:
```
Google Business: <url>
LinkedIn:        <url>
Facebook:        <url>
Alibaba:         <url>
... (jitne bhi banao)
```
