## 5. Long-term two-domain strategy (aged SilicaGelPK + newer DryGelWorld)

- **Authority transfer:** SilicaGelPK = a **minor supporting feeder** (cleaned, ≤5–6 contextual deep links). Do **not** over-interlink — Google knows they're co-owned; the real transfer comes from **third-party links** (`BACKLINK-STRATEGY.md`). Fix the hack first.
- **Brand positioning:** **DryGelWorld = the global/export authority brand**; **SilicaGelPK = the local Pakistan manufacturer brand.** Keep distinct positioning to avoid duplicate-brand confusion; the existing `sameAs` link already declares the relationship to Google.
- **Link acquisition:** prioritize **third-party** export directories, trade portals, "best-supplier" listicles, packaging/logistics media, guest posts — routed to **deep export pages**, not the homepage.
- **Content distribution:** publish authoritative content **on DryGelWorld** (the asset); reference/excerpt (not duplicate) from SilicaGelPK + social + trade profiles. **Never duplicate the same article across both domains** (cannibalization/duplicate-content risk).
