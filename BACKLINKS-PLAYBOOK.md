## How to use this playbook

1. **Work top to bottom** — listings are ordered by *expected SEO value × ease*. Don't skip ahead.
2. **One listing per session** — don't batch-paste. Each portal has slightly different fields.
3. **After each submission, log:** site name, submitted date, listing URL (once approved), and notes. A simple Google Sheet works.
4. **Confirmation emails go to** info@drygelworld.com.
5. **Categories matter more than descriptions** — pick the most specific category every time. "Silica gel manufacturer" > "Chemical supplier" > "Industrial supplier".
6. **Anchor text variation** — when the directory lets you choose the link text, rotate: "DryGelWorld", "Dry Gel World", "Silica gel manufacturer Karachi", "Industrial desiccant supplier", "Container desiccant supplier". Don't use the exact same phrase every time — Google reads this as a paid-link pattern.

## What to do AFTER the 50 listings

These compound the listings above. Plan them in month 2–3, not before.

- **NAP consistency audit** — make sure the company name, address, and phone appear *identically* across every listing. One inconsistent listing dilutes the rest.
- **Trustpilot account** — create the profile and start requesting reviews from existing buyers. 50 reviews at 4.5+ stars meaningfully shifts SERP CTR for branded searches.
- **HARO / Qwoted / SourceBottle** — answer 1 journalist query per week as "DryGelWorld export desk". Free, builds editorial backlinks from trade press over 6–12 months.
- **Guest post pitches** — Packaging Digest, Inbound Logistics, Pharmaceutical Online. Pitch case studies (anonymized buyer) not product fluff. 1 post / quarter is plenty.
- **silicagelpk.com → drygelworld.com cross-link** — if both domains are owned, add a permanent footer link from silicagelpk to drygelworld. This is the single highest-impact backlink available because you control both ends and the topical relevance is perfect.
- **LinkedIn Company Page** at `/company/drygelworld` (vs the current personal profile at `/in/drygelworld`). Company pages count toward entity signals on Knowledge Graph.

## What NOT to do (will hurt rankings)

- **Don't buy "100 backlinks for $10" packages** — these are PBNs (private blog networks) that will trigger a Google manual penalty within 6–12 months. The damage takes months to recover from.
- **Don't use the same anchor text** on every submission — looks paid.
- **Don't pay for guest posts on Fiverr** — most are on toxic domains.
- **Don't link-exchange ("you link me, I link you")** — Google's link spam algorithm flags reciprocal patterns automatically since 2022.
- **Don't claim certifications you don't hold** in any listing — buyers verify, directories revoke, and Google's E-E-A-T signal punishes the entire domain.
