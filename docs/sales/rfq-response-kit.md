# DryGelWorld — RFQ Response Kit

Reusable templates so every inquiry gets an **acknowledgement within 4 business
hours** and a **formal quotation within 24 business hours**. Copy a block, fill
the `[FILL: …]` fields, send.

> **Honesty guardrails — do NOT overclaim (kills foreign-buyer trust + is legally
> risky):**
> - Claim only **ISO 9001:2015 (manufacturer level)** and the **DMF-free
>   statement**. Nothing else is certified.
> - Say **"REACH-friendly"**, never "REACH-registered".
> - **No** FDA DMF, FDA food-contact, USP, EU 1935/2004, or pharma/food-grade
>   claims — we do not hold them.
> - **Blue indicating gel contains cobalt chloride — never call it cobalt-free.**
>   Only the orange/DMF-free indicating gel is the cobalt-free line.
> - Every number below marked `[FILL]` must be a real figure you can honour.

## 3. Freight / Incoterm cheat-sheet (internal — pick the right basis)

| Incoterm | What we quote | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| **EXW** | Factory-gate Karachi price only; buyer arranges everything | Buyers with their own freight forwarder in Pakistan |
| **FOB Karachi** | Goods cleared + loaded at Karachi port | **Default for first-time importers** — cleanest basis |
| **CIF [port]** | FOB + ocean freight + insurance to buyer's port | Buyers who want a landed-to-port number |
| **DAP [place]** | Delivered to named destination place | Buyers wanting door/port delivery, quoted per route |

- Always quote the **Incoterm + named port** together (e.g. "CIF Jebel Ali").
- Container-strip freight scales by **strip weight, route, container size,
  dispatch schedule** — get these before quoting CargoDry.
- First-order risk controls to offer proactively (builds trust): pre-shipment
  inspection option, [FILL: bank details matching the legal entity name],
  shipment insurance on CIF.
