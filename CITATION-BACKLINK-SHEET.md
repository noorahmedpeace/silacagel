Movement on **winnable keywords** ("silica gel manufacturer Karachi", "container desiccant supplier Pakistan", "silica gel exporter Pakistan") realistically shows in **6–12 weeks** as these get crawled.

- The marketplaces (Tier 1) may bring **RFQs before rankings even move** — that's the real near-term win. Treat them as a sales channel, not just SEO.
