## 1. Executive Summary

DryGelWorld has moved from a basic manufacturer website into a strong B2B SEO and AI-search asset. The technical foundation is better than most small and mid-market desiccant suppliers: server-rendered pages, clean crawlability, live `llms.txt`, AI crawler allowance, product/category/export pages, comparison content, calculators, case studies, documents, pricing, and a large sitemap.

The next bottleneck is no longer basic technical SEO. The next bottleneck is **authority, proof, uniqueness, and conversion instrumentation**. Global competitors and directories still win on age, trust, citations, product breadth, third-party proof, and backlinks. DryGelWorld can win narrower commercial markets by being the clearest manufacturer-direct Pakistan/export source for silica gel sachets, bulk beads, container strips, private-label packets, and documentation-backed moisture control.
