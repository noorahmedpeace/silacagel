# DryGelWorld — Backlinks & Authority Execution Plan

**Why this is the #1 priority:** the site's on-page SEO is already strong (topical clusters, comparison content, clean metadata, structured data). GSC shows content ranking at positions 5–16 with impressions but few clicks. That profile = a **new, low-authority domain**. The lever that moves it is **backlinks + citations** (Google trust), not more on-page tweaks. Do this consistently for 8–12 weeks and rankings climb.

**Rules:** use the SAME business name, address, phone (NAP) everywhere. Always link to `https://www.drygelworld.com`. Quality > quantity — a few relevant links beat dozens of spammy ones.

---

## WEEK 1 — Foundation citations (fast, also drive direct leads) ⭐
These are quick, high-trust, and several are B2B lead sources too. Do all of them.

- [ ] **Google Business Profile** — create/claim, category "Packaging supply store" / "Manufacturer", add photos, website, WhatsApp. (Biggest local-trust signal.)
- [ ] **Bing Places** — same details.
- [ ] **B2B marketplaces** (also lead-gen — real buyers search here):
  - [ ] Alibaba (Gold Supplier if budget allows; free listing otherwise)
  - [ ] Made-in-China.com
  - [ ] TradeKey, ExportHub, EC21, Global Sources
  - [ ] IndiaMART (buyers in the region source here)
- [ ] **Pakistan export / trade bodies** (strong .gov / .org authority):
  - [ ] TDAP (Trade Development Authority of Pakistan) exporter listing
  - [ ] Karachi Chamber of Commerce & Industry (KCCI) member directory
  - [ ] FPCCI directory
- [ ] **General business directories:** Kompass, Europages, Yellow Pages Pakistan.

> Each of these is a link + a citation + (for the marketplaces) a lead channel. This alone builds the base authority a new site needs.

## ONGOING (every week) — the compounding links
- [ ] **HARO / Qwoted / Featured** (journalist request services): sign up, filter for "packaging", "shipping", "moisture", "supply chain", "manufacturing" queries. Reply with a genuine expert quote 2–3×/week. These land links on high-authority news/blog sites. **Highest-value ongoing tactic.**
- [ ] **Quora + Reddit** (r/logistics, r/AskEngineers, packaging/ecommerce subs): answer real questions about desiccants/moisture with a genuinely helpful answer, link a relevant blog post where it truly helps. (No spam — value first.)
- [ ] **Guest posts / expert contributions**: pitch 1 article/month to packaging, export, or e-commerce-fulfilment blogs (topic ideas below). Byline links back.
- [ ] **Promote your linkable assets** — you already have two magnets:
  - The **Container Desiccant Calculator** (`/tools/container-desiccant-calculator`) and **Moisture Load Calculator**. Get these listed on packaging/logistics "useful tools/resources" pages — free tools earn links naturally. Email 5 relevant resource-page owners/month.
  - Your comparison guides (silica vs clay, vs molecular sieve, vs oxygen absorber) — pitch as references.

## Track it
- GSC → **Links** report (see referring domains grow).
- Aim: **+5–10 quality referring domains/month**. At ~30–40 good referring domains, you'll see page-2 pages (like the toxic-safety guide) move toward page 1.

## Honest timeline
Foundation links (Week 1) show benefit in a few weeks. Meaningful ranking/traffic lift from authority takes **2–4 months of consistent effort**. For customers *now*, run **Google Ads** on high-intent terms (e.g. "silica gel supplier", "container desiccant supplier") in parallel — SEO is the long game, ads are the short game.
